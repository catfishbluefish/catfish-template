about us

catfish.bluefish is a small web design and development company created by Cat Fistori based out of the San Francisco Bay Area. After working full time as a web designer, product photographer and onsite SEO manager for a Shopify based e-commerce store Beadshop.com, Cat decided to start her own web development company. Too often she found people and companies juggling multiple contractors to complete their websites - someone to code it, someone to maintain it, someone to make design changes and decisions, marketing companies, product photographers, SEO researchers, graphic designers, and more.

But what if there was a company that could do all of that? Just one company to take you from an idea- to a design, complete with custom graphics and logos - develop and publish it, maintain it, set up on-site SEO, set up social media platforms, and even take your product photos!

Well, that is the goal here at catfish.bluefish, whether you need all of our services or just one - we are here to help you simplify your website experience.
